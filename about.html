About me

Hi, I am Cecilia Wang

My Chinese name is Xinran (pronounced Cin-Ran), I am a UX designer who previously worked in Animation and Motion graphics design. I have a passion for problem solving and design strategy. I am a curious soul with a strong interest in learning people’s behavior, emotions, and attitudes. Growing up in China and living in Finland have given me unique perspectives on how to relate, to think, to question, and to broaden my view of possibilities in life.

After receving my Master's I spent time freelancing web and motion design while looking for a new direction, meanwhile I worked for clients such as naaG (Finland’s biggest men’s fashion), Bright data (web data platform), Aroma garden (Essential oil ecommerce). This has lead to finding the path of UX, it has given me the opportunities to converge my broad range of creative interests and put them into good use to create values.
